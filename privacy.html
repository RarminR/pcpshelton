Privacy & HIPAA

We respect your privacy and follow HIPAA guidelines to protect your information.

Your Privacy Rights

As a patient, you have rights regarding your health information:

  • You may request copies of your medical records via the Patient Portal
  • We only share information with your consent or when required by law
  • Your information is kept confidential in accordance with HIPAA regulations
