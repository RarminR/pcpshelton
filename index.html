Patient Portal - Our Preferred Way to Connect

For the quickest and most accurate service, please use our secure Patient Portal for routine questions, prescription refills, and test results. All portal messages are documented in your chart for safer, more coordinated care.

Our staff and providers monitor messages during the week and on weekends. Depending on the nature of your concern, you will receive a response within the appropriate time frame.

NCQA Patient-Centered Medical Home

Recognized for excellence in team-based, coordinated care

Our practice has proudly maintained NCQA Patient-Centered Medical Home recognition for five consecutive years. This credential means we've met rigorous national standards for team-based care, care coordination, and continuous quality improvement.

We're among approximately 13,000 practices nationwide committed to this model, which research shows improves quality and patient satisfaction while reducing healthcare costs. It's our commitment to maintaining the highest standards of primary care, every year.

Patient Portal - Our Preferred Communication Method

We encourage all our patients to use the Patient Portal as the primary way to communicate with our office.

Using the portal ensures your messages are documented accurately and allows our care team to respond promptly and efficiently to your needs. Phone calls, while available, can sometimes be missed or misunderstood, leading to delays or errors in communication and documentation.

We apologize if our phone system has ever been confusing or felt like a hassle. The benefit of the current phone system is that all calls are securely cataloged in your chart, which helps prevent mishaps and ensures the best possible care for you.

For the fastest and most accurate assistance, please use the Patient Portal for routine requests, questions, and updates. Our team is always ready to help guide you through using the portal if you need any assistance.

Thank you for helping us provide safer, more reliable healthcare.
