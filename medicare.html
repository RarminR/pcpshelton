Medicare, Medicare Advantage & Medicaid (HUSKY) Policy

At this time, Primary Care of Shelton is not accepting new patients insured through Medicare, Medicare Advantage, or Medicaid (HUSKY).

This policy does not affect existing patients, who remain active in the practice.
